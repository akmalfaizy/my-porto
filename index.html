Tourism App Sleman Wanderlust

Sleman Wanderlust App is an Android-based mobile application I developed as my undergraduate thesis project at Universitas Teknologi Yogyakarta. The app enhances the travel experience in Sleman by offering key features such as destination recommendations using the Haversine formula to calculate the nearest attractions, tourist ticket booking and validation with payment proof and QR code, local culinary exploration across various categories, real-time navigation, bug reporting, and user account management. Built using the Agile methodology, the app not only ensures reliable functionality but also promotes sustainable tourism by highlighting eco-friendly destinations and supporting local culinary businesses.

FitSphere - Fitness Application

FitSphere is a fitness application designed with two roles: Member and Admin. Members can purchase memberships and fitness classes through an integrated Midtrans payment gateway, access the latest fitness news, and display a QR code after purchasing a membership or class to verify entry at the fitness center. The app also provides a BMI calculator that offers personalized suggestions through a pop-up, a feature to choose fitness consultants with direct access via WhatsApp, and an exercise tracker to record workout progress. For admins, FitSphere provides tools to manage classes, memberships, and fitness news, as well as to oversee registered consultants. Admins can also generate transaction reports for membership and class purchases in daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly summaries. Additionally, the system allows admins to scan member QR codes to verify purchased memberships or classes. With these features, FitSphere creates a seamless digital ecosystem that supports both members and administrators in managing their fitness activities effectively.
